About Us

Who We Are ?

We are a trading and producing company for a variety of fruits and vegetables exported to you directly from Egypt, we work with you one on one to pick, sort, package and ship the product to your exact needs.Scince our goal is your full satisfaction, we pay full attention to all details and client instructions.

Mango , Orange , Sweet Potato , Red Onion, Golden Onion, Garlic, Butternut Pumpkin , Pumpkin are our speciality, most of our products are GG and FT certified, Organic Certification available for some products.

Please send us your requests for any additional info regarding specific product, we are happy to answer all your questions. Weather you need a one time quote or an annual contract we are looking forward to fulfilling your needs.
